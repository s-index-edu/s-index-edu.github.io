How S-Index is calculated

  • For a conference, we count how many papers each author has published in that conference since its inception. The S-Index is the largest number k such that at least k authors have each published at least k papers in the conference.

  • The S-Index set of a conference with S-Index k is the set of authors with at least k publications in that conference. The size of this set can exceed k.

  • The S-i-Index of a conference is the number of authors who have published at least i papers in that conference.
